ProFest

IEEE ProFest is the annual event where the best projects made by the students of SEAS are showcased to newly admitted students and public. ProFest (2016) is the first project festival of School of Engineering and Applied Science.


The deadline for submission of the projects is 28th June.

Women in Engineering Group


Gone are the days of gender discrepancy in the emerging fields of engineering. Ahmedabad University Students' Branch of IEEE, with an immense ebullience, declares the establishment of the Women In Engineering group of IEEE, AU. Along with great zeal and enthusiasm, the branch has planned events and activities, in order to enhance and encourage the participation of women in ameliorating the society. With the establishment of WIE group, the AU SB of IEEE looks forward for the betterment, in the times ahead.

Welcome to the Ahmedabad University Students' Branch of IEEE


The Ahmedabad University Students' Branch of IEEE thrives to have a practical approach towards engineering and sufficient exposure of the growing developments, by becoming a part of the world's largest technical professional society, IEEE.

Why Join IEEE ?

As a member of IEEE, you will be presented with -

  • New resources, valuable opportunities
  • Many discounts that will help you advance your career in the right direction
  • Stay updated with the latest news from IEEE, IEEE Spectrum, IEEE standards News.
  • Access to all technical papers.
  • Access all IEEE journal articles as well as conference papers.
  • Learn from courses available at IEEE eLearning Library.
  • Get connected with local IEEE Section.
  • Give Exposure to your talents and carve your way out leading to a successful career.
  • Get national as well as international exposure in your field of interest.
  • Get chance to represent your Institue at various events, competitions, workshops,etc. organised by IEEE Student Branches.
  • Get the leverage of a variety of scholarships, grants, fellowship programs, internships for IEEE Student members.
  • Enjoy various forms of discounts for IEEE Student members.
